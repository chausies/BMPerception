Welcome! You will be given a series of 96 tasks (part 1), a quick survey (part 2), and then another series of 96 tasks (part 3). The whole process should take only about 25 minutes.

Instructions for part 1

For each task, you will see a black figure walking left or right for a few seconds. As fast as you can during this time, press the left arrow key (←) if you think it's moving left or the right arrow key (→) if you think it's moving right. Soon after, you will briefly see a white figure either walking or running. After this, please choose whether you think it was walking or running. You will do 96 tasks like this.

Lastly, throughout each task, there will be a red dot in the center of the screen. Please keep your eyes focused on that red dot at all times while doing the tasks.

When you've understood the instructions and are ready to begin, please click the "Ready" button. After 1 example task, you'll go straight to doing the 96 tasks of part 1.
